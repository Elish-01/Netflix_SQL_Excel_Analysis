#Netflix content Analysis Project Using SQL and Excel
##Overview
  -This project analyzes Netflix content to find the content growth, genre distribution, country wise production, and the ratio of the Netflix content of Movies vs TV Show
  -SQL is used for data manipulation, filtering and data transformation by writing queries while excel is used for data visualization and dashboards.

##Source
 -Dateset:- Netflix Movies and TV Show Content
 -Source:- kaggle

##Tools Used
 -SQLite
 -Excel
 -SQL
 -Pivot Chart

##Problems Solved
 -top Country to produce highest no. of contents
 -top genre which dominate the field
 -the year when netflix content growth accelerated rapidly
 -percentage of Netflix content of Movies vs Tv show

##Key insights
 -Netflix content growth accelerated rapids after 2015
 -International Movies dominate the genre with 1797 titles produced in between 2008 and 2019.
 -The US contributes the highest number of content.
 -Movies account for about 67% of total content.

##Business recommendation
 -continue investment in international content as of their rapid growth trend
 -Produce more regional content in emerging markets like India and southkorea
 -Increase genre diversification to attract wider audiences.
 -Use viewer analytics to strengthen documentary and TV shows engagement.
